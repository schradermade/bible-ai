# V1 Feature Scope

## 1. Goal
Deliver a web-first, AI-centered Bible study companion that proves users trust the app to understand Scripture and think biblically about life.

---

## 2. In Scope (V1)

### 2.1 Core Product
- Account system (email + password)
- Scripture reader with basic navigation
- Verse highlighting and basic notes
- Saved AI responses

### 2.2 AI Experiences (Primary Differentiator)
- AI-guided verse explanation
- AI-guided life discernment
- AI-guided encouragement
- AI-guided prophecy introduction (non-speculative)
- AI-guided journaling (light, optional)

### 2.3 UX Expectations
- AI is visible, guided, and structured (no blank chat box)
- AI responses follow `docs/AI_UX_BEHAVIOR_SPEC.md`
- Scripture citations appear in every AI response
- Clear disclaimers about AI limits during onboarding

---

## 3. Out of Scope (V1)

- Community, comments, or social features
- Live news analysis or article ingestion
- Denominational settings or theological presets
- Deep original language tooling (lexicons, parsing)
- Mobile app or offline support
- AI autonomy or proactive interventions

---

## 4. Success Metrics

- Users report the AI feels helpful, calm, and respectful of Scripture
- 30-day retention for core study users (target to define)
- Meaningful usage of at least one AI feature per session
- Low confusion about AI authority or boundaries

---

## 5. Key Risks

- AI tone drift or overconfidence
- V1 scope creep into community or news analysis
- Insufficient clarity about AI limits during onboarding
